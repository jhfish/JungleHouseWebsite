About the Property

Where the Wild Meets the Wonderful

Tucked along the iconic Jungle Trail in Vero Beach, our brand-new bardominium blends the raw beauty of Florida's Indian River Lagoon with thoughtful luxury design. Built for large groups who want space, privacy, and serious amenities — without sacrificing comfort.

Five bedrooms spread across an expansive steel-frame build that's both hurricane-resilient and beautifully appointed. Step outside to 4 lush acres of Florida landscape, a sweeping wrap-around front porch, and a private dock for access to the Intracoastal Waterway.

  • 🏠 Newly constructed bardominium
  • 🌳 4 private acres on Jungle Trail
  • ⚓ Private dock & Intracoastal access
  • 🌈 Vero Beach's most sought-after corridor

Explore Vero Beach

Local Favorites Near Jungle Trail

Jungle Trail puts you at the heart of one of Florida's most charming coastal towns. Here's what's nearby:

🍽 Dining

  • Waldo's Restaurant Iconic oceanfront dining at the historic Driftwood Resort — casual Florida vibes, seafood & cocktails.
  • Citrus Grillhouse Upscale Florida cuisine with local citrus-inspired dishes and a stellar wine list.
  • Ocean Grill A Vero Beach institution since 1941 — fresh seafood with panoramic Atlantic views.
  • Marsh Island Bar & Grill Waterfront spot right on the Indian River. Casual lunches and laid-back dinners.
  • Nino's Café Beloved local Italian spot for hearty homemade pastas and friendly service.

🌊 Beaches & Outdoors

  • Jungle Trail A protected scenic byway on your doorstep — biking, birding, and some of Florida's most untouched beauty.
  • Treasure Shores Beach Park Uncrowded Atlantic beach just minutes away — great for shelling and sunrise swims.
  • Oslo Riverfront Conservation Area Kayaking and paddleboarding trails through the Indian River Lagoon.
  • Pelican Island National Wildlife Refuge America's first national wildlife refuge, right in your backyard. Birdwatchers paradise.

🎆 Activities & Arts

  • McKee Botanical Garden Award-winning tropical gardens and a peaceful retreat just a short drive away.
  • Vero Beach Museum of Art A top-tier regional art museum featuring rotating and permanent collections.
  • Riverside Theatre Professional live theatre in a stunning intimate venue — great for a night out.
  • Quail Valley River Club Golf, tennis, and spa experiences available for members and guests.

🍶 Drinks & Nightlife

  • Cobalt Restaurant & Bar Rooftop cocktails with sweeping ocean views — the best sunset bar on the Treasure Coast.
  • Indian River Brewing Company Local craft brewery with rotating taps and a fun taproom atmosphere.
  • The Tides Oceanfront patio bar — frozen drinks, live music, and salt air.
  • Mulligan's Beach House Classic Florida beach bar with live entertainment and great wings.
